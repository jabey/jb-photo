Privacy Policy

We value and respect your privacy. On this page we outline the privacy policy for www.jonathanball.photography

Personal Information

The only time we store any information about you is if you use the Contact page to send us a message. In this case we will collect the following information:

  • name
  • email address
  • miscellaneous information you may supply in the message detail

We will only use this information to respond to your enquiry.

If you have ticked the box to be added to our mailing list we may periodically send promotional emails about new products and services, special offers or other information which we think you may find interesting using the email address which you have provided.

We will never pass any of your details to any third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so.
